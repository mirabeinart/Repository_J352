Photojournalist in the Making

Mira Beinart

As a journalism student at Phillip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland, sophomore Mira Beinart has thoroughly enjoyed taking JOUR370: Photojournalism. She took videography last semester, which inspired her passion for cameras and taking pictures. As soon as she picked up the camera and took her first photos, her life was never the same.

Below, you can find some of Mira's favorite pictures that she's taken for her photojournalism class, ranging in topics from people to places to things.
